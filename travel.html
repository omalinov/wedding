Welcome Drink

The wedding venue is located 5 km from the church. For your convenience, the transportation from Plovdiv will take all guests directly from the church to the venue. Upon arrival, you'll be welcomed with a selection of drinks and light bites — including a variety of cold alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

Accomodation

Guests will be staying at two locations: one at the wedding venue and the other in downtown Perushtitsa. For those staying in Perushtitsa, we've arranged transportation running from 22:00 to 04:00, with scheduled trips from the venue to the guest houses every hour. If you need an additional trip outside the schedule, just speak with the driver — they'll be happy to assist. Check-in for overnight stays begins after 14:00, and payment for the room will be made upon arrival. Checkout is at 11:00 on September 13th.
